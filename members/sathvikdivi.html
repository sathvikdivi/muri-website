Sathvik Divi

PhD Student, Microrobotics Lab,
Mechanical Engineering, Carnegie Mellon University

Sathvik Divi is a PhD student in the Microrobotics Lab (PI: Prof. Sarah Bergbreiter) at Carnegie Mellon University. His research focuses on using latch-mediation in small-scale jumping robots especially in compliant environments. With a background in mechanical engineering, he loves working on the design and control aspects of small-scale robots. He has a BS and MS in Mechanical Engineering from Bits Pilani (India) and NC State University respectively. He is an avid soccer fan and loves to play whenever he can.
