Dr. Jeffrey Olberding

Postdoctoral Researcher, UC Irvine

Jeff is a postdoctoral researcher in the Azizi Lab in the Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at UC Irvine. His research focuses on the integrative musculoskeletal physiology underlying organismal movement, including LaMSA. He studies how the interactions between muscles, connective tissues, skeletons, and the environment result in movement, asks how these movements can be controlled to produce context-dependent performance, and explores the evolution of these systems.
