Fast Movements, Impacts and Deformations:
Nature, Robotics and Materials

Impuslive MURI Symposium | July 19, 2022 - July 21, 2022 | The Washington Duke Inn; Durham, NC USA

Overview

Our objectives for this symposium are to:

  • Address fundamental principles behind small, fast, repeatable biological and engineered systems
  • Foster multidisciplinary community and communication across fields
  • Establish the field as an inclusive, diverse group of individuals spanning multiple fields across institutions
We welcome all scientists and engineers, and we see specific opportunities for biologists working on LaMSA (Latch-Mediated Spring Actuation) systems; engineers focused on robotics, high-rate actuating devices, and high acceleration dynamics; and materials scientists focused on high rate-deformation, resilience, protective dissipation and healing.
